# VoiceFlow Marketing Kit

## Positioning

VoiceFlow is a Windows dictation assistant for people who write all day. Hold a
hotkey, speak naturally, and VoiceFlow pastes clean text into the app you are
already using.

## One-Liner

Fast voice-to-text for Windows, with AI cleanup and clipboard commands.

## Short Description

VoiceFlow turns your voice into polished text anywhere on Windows. Use local
dictation for quick notes, then upgrade to Plus for AI cleanup, rewrites,
summaries, translations, and voice commands that work with copied text.

## Target Users

- Founders, creators, and freelancers who write emails, posts, and docs.
- Developers who want quick voice prompts and code-edit commands.
- Students and knowledge workers who capture notes faster by speaking.
- Teams that need a lightweight Windows dictation tool without changing apps.

## Core Benefits

- Speak in any app: email, browser, chat, docs, IDEs, and note tools.
- Local dictation works without an AI key.
- AI cleanup removes filler, fixes punctuation, and keeps meaning intact.
- Clipboard commands summarize, rewrite, translate, and edit copied text.
- The floating orb keeps the workflow visible without taking over the screen.
